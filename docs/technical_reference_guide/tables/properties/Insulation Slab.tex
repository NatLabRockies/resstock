{Name & Units & Description}

\texttt{enclosure\_slab\_perimeter\_insulation\_nominal\_r\_value} & F-ft2-hr/Btu & Nominal R-value of the vertical slab perimeter insulation.  \\ \hline
\texttt{enclosure\_slab\_perimeter\_insulation\_depth} & ft & Depth from grade to bottom of vertical slab perimeter insulation.  \\ \hline
\texttt{enclosure\_slab\_thickness} & in & The thickness of the slab. Zero can be entered if there is a dirt floor instead of a slab.  \\ \hline
\texttt{enclosure\_slab\_carpet\_fraction} & frac & Fraction of the slab floor area that is carpeted.  \\ \hline
\texttt{enclosure\_slab\_carpet\_r\_value} & F-ft2-hr/Btu & R-value of the slab carpet.  \\
