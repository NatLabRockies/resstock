\paragraph{Description}
% \input{characteristics/description/Electric Vehicle Charger}
Presence and type of electric vehicle charger used at the dwelling unit.

\paragraph{Distribution Data Sources}
% \input{characteristics/sources/Electric Vehicle Charger}
U.S. EIA 2020 Residential Energy Consumption Survey (RECS) microdata.

\input{characteristics/dependencies/Electric Vehicle Charger}

\paragraph{Distribution Assumptions}
% \input{characteristics/assumptions/Electric Vehicle Charger}
Dwelling units with an electric vehicle need to have at least a Level 1 charger. Due to low sample sizes, coarsening of the dependencies are applied in the following order.
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Federal Poverty Level lumped every 100\%
  \item Federal Poverty Level lumped every 200\%
  \item Building type is consolidated into three bins 1) single-family detached and 2) multi-family with 5+ units, and 3) mobile homes, multi-family 2-4 units, and single-family attached
  \item Building type is consolidated into two bins 1) single-family detached and 2) all other building types
  \item Federal Poverty Level is combined into 400\%+ and <400\% bins
\end{enumerate}
