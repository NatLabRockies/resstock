\begin{itemize}
\item The data obtained is from surveyed oil and gas well data.
\item The latitude and longitudes were assigned to counties and the data was joined to the ResStock spatial lookup tables. In this process, 1482 of 59332 samples did not have a FIPS match or did not have data and were dropped.
\item Due to limited data in climate zone 1A, data was pulled from samples in 1A plus Florida 2A.
\item Samples less than 0.5 Btu/hr-ft-F are assigned a value of 0.5 Btu/hr-ft-F. Samples greater than 2.6 Btu/hr-ft-F are assigned a value of 2.6 Btu/hr-ft-F.
\end{itemize}
