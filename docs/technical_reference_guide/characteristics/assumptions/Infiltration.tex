\begin{itemize}
\item All ACH50 are based on Single-Family Detached blower door tests.
\item Climate zones that are copied: 2A to 1A, 6A to 7A, and 6B to 7B.
\item Vintage bins that are copied: 2000s to 2010s, 1950s to 1940s, 1950s to <1940s.
\item Homes are assumed to not be Weatherization Assistance Program (WAP) qualified and not ENERGY STAR certified.
\item Climate zones 7AK and 8AK are averages of 6A and 6B.
\item ResStock models multi-family and SFA units with the unit total air leakage type. The unit total air leakage assume that some of the sampled ACH50 value goes to neighboring units. The model infiltration value to the exterior is a smaller infiltration value that what is sampled and is adjusted by the ratio of exterior envelope surface area to total envelope surface area. The modeled infiltration to the exterior is reported in the results.
\item For Alaska, we are using a field in ARIS that lumps multi-family 2-4 units and multi-family 5+ units buildings together. Data from the American Community Survey is used to distribute the between these two building types.
\item For Alaska, Infiltration ACH50 values are calculated based on CFM50 from blower door test and estimated volume of the home.
\end{itemize}
