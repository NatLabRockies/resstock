\paragraph{Description}
% \input{characteristics/description/Income RECS2020}
Income of the household occupying the housing unit that are aligned
with the 2020 U.S. Energy Information Administration Residential Energy
Consumption Survey.

\paragraph{Distribution Data Sources}
% \input{characteristics/sources/Income RECS2020}
\begin{itemize} 
\item
  2019 5-year PUMS from the University of Minnesota.
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Direct Conditional Dependencies}
\input{characteristics/dependencies/Income RECS2020}

\paragraph{Options}
The Income RECS2020 options are bins mapped from the Income characteristic to align with the RECS 2020 income bins. The characteristic does not set any modeling properties.

\paragraph{Distribution Assumptions}
% \input{characteristics/assumptions/Income RECS2020}
\begin{itemize}
\item
  The income in 2019 USD are consolidated to align with those of RECS 2020 without inflation adjustment.
\end{itemize}
