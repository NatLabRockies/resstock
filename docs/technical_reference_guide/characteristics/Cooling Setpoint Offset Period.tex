\paragraph{Options}
The options shown in Table \ref{table:hc_opt_cooling_setpoint_offset_period} combine day and night offset periods. The default for the day is from 9 AM to 5 PM and for the night is 10 PM to 7 AM. The options then shift these periods randomly up to 5 hours in either direction. The shifting of the periods is mainly to avoid the synchronization of HVAC systems all turning on and off simultaneously. The property values are hourly arrays for when the setup occurs (a value of 1), a setback (a value of -1), and when the setup or setback does not occur (a value of 0).
