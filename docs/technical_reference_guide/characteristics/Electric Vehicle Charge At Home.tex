\paragraph{Options}
The options shown in Table \ref{table:hc_opt_electric_vehicle_charge_at_home} for the Electric Vehicle Charge At Home characteristic determine how much of the electric vehicle's energy shows up on the residential meter vs charging at work or at public chargers. Every unit in the baseline is assigned one of these options so that the home charging fraction does not need to be sampled during an electric vehicle upgrade. The units with an electric vehicle are specified by the Electric Vehicle Ownership characteristic.
