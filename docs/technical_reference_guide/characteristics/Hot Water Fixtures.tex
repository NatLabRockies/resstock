\paragraph{Distribution Assumptions}
% \input{characteristics/assumptions/Hot Water Fixtures}
\begin{itemize}
\item
  A log-normal distribution was shown to match the distribution of annual
  energy consumption.
\item
  The data shows appoximately a log-normal distribution. From the data, the average multiplier is 0.8 and the
  standard deviation is 0.2. The average multiplier was mean-shifted from 0.8 to 1 average usage.
\item
  Low, Medium, and High usage is assigned based on the lower 25th
  percent, middle 50th percent, and upper 25th percent.
\end{itemize}
